Frequently Asked Questions

What is EzMoney?

EzMoney is a fintech application that provides real-time stock prices and comprehensive company information through a user-friendly interface. Our goal is to make financial data accessible to everyone, from beginners to professional investors.

How does the app work?

Our app connects to reliable financial data sources and presents the information in an easy-to-understand format. You can search for specific stocks, view their real-time prices, access company information, and track your favorite investments all in one place.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We implement bank-level encryption and security measures to protect all user data. We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security.
